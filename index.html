Quality Control & Assurance

DECO is ISO certified

 

  • ISO 9001:2015
  • ISO 14001:2015
  • ISO 45001:2018

ISO 9001:2015

We believe in the importance of the development and implementation of quality management systems for improving our ability to:

  • Consistently deliver services/products that meet customer, and
  • Meet applicable statutory and regulatory, requirements; and
  • Address customer satisfaction through the effective application of the quality system, including continual improvement and the prevention of non-conformity.

ISO 14001:2015

With Deco commitment to “sense of social responsibility”, we believe of the importance of the environmental management systems (EMS) , and how the environmental thinking can impact on reducing costs and improve efficiency within the organization, by adopting EMS we have the ability in:

  • Cost savings in waste, recycling and consumption.
  • Management of environmental risks.
  • Compliance with environmental regulations.
  • Increase employee engagement in the knowledge of working in an environmentally friendly organization.

ISO 45001:2018

Deco recognizes that the best way to maximize manpower- efficiency is to improve safety in the workplace. We comply with the occupational health and safety (OH&S) management system, to make sure providing safe and healthy workplaces by preventing work-related injury and ill health.

OH&S is part of employees’ culture, along with a quality system manual and company internal rules & regulations. In this regard, Deco is committed to help its employees in maintaining a safe and healthy workplace and promoting safe work practices.
